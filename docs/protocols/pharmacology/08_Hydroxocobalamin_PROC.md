# Hydroxocobalamin
**Trade Name:** CYANOKIT

## Classification & Action
- **Drug Class:** Cyanide Antidote
- **Mechanism of Action:** Binds Cyanide ions for excretion in urine

## Indications
- Exposure to combustion products / fire in an enclosed space with altered mental status, seizures, hypotension and/or cardiac arrest

## Contraindications
- None in emergency use

## Precautions
- Requires reconstitution (**200 mL** **0.9% Sodium Chloride**)
- Use vented IV tubing
- May increase blood pressure: Caution in patients with severe hypertension
