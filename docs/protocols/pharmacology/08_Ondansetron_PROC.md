# **[Ondansetron](../pharmacology/Ondansetron.md)**
**Trade Name:** [Ondansetron](../pharmacology/Ondansetron.md)

## Classification & Action
- **Drug Class:** Antiemetic
- **Mechanism of Action:** Relieves nausea and vomiting

## Indications
- Nausea and vomiting
- Pain management (prophylactic)

## Contraindications
- Allergy/hypersensitivity
- Prolonged QTc (>500ms)

## Precautions
- Less effective if used after vomiting has begun
