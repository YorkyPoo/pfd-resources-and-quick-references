# **[Glucagon](../pharmacology/Glucagon.md)**
**Trade Name:** Not specified in protocol

## Classification & Action
- **Drug Class:** Hormone
- **Mechanism of Action:** Breaks down stored glycogen to glucose, raising blood glucose level. Increases heart rate/cardiac contractility (CCB overdose).

## Indications
- Hypoglycemia
- Symptomatic Calcium channel blocker (CCB) overdose
- Symptomatic Beta blocker (BB) overdose

## Contraindications
- None in emergency use

## Precautions
- Requires adequate glycogen stores to be effective.
- May be less effective or ineffective in patients with a history of alcoholism, chronic liver disease, and/or malnutrition as well as low birth weight neonates/infants.
