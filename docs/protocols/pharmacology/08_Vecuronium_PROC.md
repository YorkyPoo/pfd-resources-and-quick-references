# Vecuronium
**Trade Name:** Norcuron

## Classification & Action
- **Drug Class:** Non-depolarizing neuromuscular blocker / paralytic
- **Mechanism of Action:** Rapidly binds to acetylcholine receptors leading to reversible paralysis (75-90 seconds)

## Indications
- Rapid sequence induction

## Contraindications
- Known hypersensitivity / allergy

## Precautions
- PARALYZING AGENT. PATIENT MUST BE VENTILATED.
- Anesthetic / induction agent must be given prior to paralytic when performing RSI.
- Duration of action may be prolonged in patients with severe hypocalcemia/hypokalemia/hypermagnesemia, cachexia, acidosis, or myasthenia gravis.
- Duration of action is longer than that of available sedatives / induction agents. Provide adequate analgesia / sedation to ensure patient safety and comfort.
