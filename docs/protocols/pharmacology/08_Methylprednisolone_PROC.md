# Methylprednisolone
**Trade Name:** Solu-Medrol

## Classification & Action
- **Drug Class:** Synthetic corticosteroid
- **Mechanism of Action:** Reduces inflammation/bronchial edema

## Indications
- Allergic reaction / Anaphylaxis
- Respiratory distress

## Contraindications
- Known hypersensitivity / allergy
- Recent myocardial infarction
- Sepsis/septic shock

## Precautions
- May exacerbate glaucoma, myasthenia gravis, hyperglycemia, and fluid retention due to CHF, kidney or liver disease. Use with caution in these patients and only when benefit outweighs risk.
