# **Etomidate**
**Trade Name:** Amidate

## Classification & Action
- **Drug Class:** General anesthetic
- **Mechanism of Action:** Rapidly induces anesthesia

## Indications
- Rapid sequence induction

## Contraindications
- Known hypersensitivity / allergy

## Precautions
- Adrenal insufficiency
- May increase the risk of post-intubation hypotension
- Has no analgesic properties

## Dosage
### Adults
- **Not specified in protocol**

### Pediatrics
- **Not specified in protocol**

## Pharmacokinetics
| Onset | Duration |
| :--- | :--- |
| 30 – 60 seconds | 5 – 10 minutes |

## Side Effects
- Hypotension
- Myoclonus
- Nausea / vomiting
- Nystagmus
- Local irritation

## Interactions
- Not specified in protocol

## Pearls
- ⚠ **Warning:** May increase the risk of post-intubation hypotension. Has no analgesic properties.
