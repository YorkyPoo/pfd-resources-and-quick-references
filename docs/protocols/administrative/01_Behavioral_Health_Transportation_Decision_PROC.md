# Poquoson Fire and Rescue Patient Care Protocols
## Administrative - Behavioral Health Transportation Decision

### Overview
Appropriately identify patients experiencing a mental health crisis that can benefit from direct transport to a behavioral health facility.

### Assessment & Criteria
=== "History of Present Illness"
    - Suicidal Ideations / Homicidal Ideations
    - Seeking Substance abuse help
    - Mood disorder (Depression / Anxiety)
    - Psychosis / Schizophrenia / Bipolar

=== "Signs & Symptoms"
    - Is patient oriented to person, place and time?
    - Confirms prior psych history?

=== "Differential Considerations"
    - No concern for infectious process?
    - No indications of intentional overdose?


### Interventions
| # | Action / Intervention | EMR | EMT | A | I | P |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **Assess decision-making capacity** | | • | | • | |
| 2 | **Direct Transport:** Once identified, consider direct transport to a Mental Health Facility. | | • | | • | |
| 3 | **Medical Control:** When in doubt regarding appropriate destination or patient status, contact Medical Control. | | • | | • | |
| 4 | **Psychiatric Emergencies:** Acute psychiatric emergencies with significant risk of harm to self/others refusing treatment/transport: contact law enforcement regarding Emergency Custody Order (ECO) for psychiatric evaluation. | | • | | • | |
| 5 | **Medical Emergencies:** Acute medical emergencies lacking decision-making capacity refusing treatment/transport: contact Medical Control regarding ECO for medical evaluation. MC must petition a magistrate. Contact law enforcement to assist. | | • | | • | |
| 6 | **Law Enforcement Custody:** Patients in LE custody (including ECOs) or under arrest **MUST** have a law enforcement officer present at all times. | | • | | • | |
| 7 | **Restraints:** If LE restraints utilized (handcuffs, flex-cuffs), LE officer **SHOULD** accompany the patient. | | • | | • | |

### Pearls
* ⚠ **Medical vs. Psychiatric ECO:** A Psychiatric ECO is obtained by Law Enforcement for psychiatric evaluations. A Medical ECO requires Medical Control to petition a magistrate for patients lacking decision-making capacity due to medical emergencies.
* ⚠ Patients under Law Enforcement custody or an ECO MUST have a law enforcement officer present at all times.
* ⚠ If Law Enforcement restraints are used (e.g., handcuffs), the officer SHOULD accompany the patient in the ambulance.
