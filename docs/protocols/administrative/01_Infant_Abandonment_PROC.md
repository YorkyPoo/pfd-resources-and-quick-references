# Poquoson Fire and Rescue Patient Care Protocols
## Administrative - Infant Abandonment (Safe Haven)

### Overview
Procedures for receiving a surrendered infant under the Virginia Safe Haven Law (Reference §18.2-371, §18.2-371.1, §40.1-103). Providers in the Poquoson Fire and Rescue region will follow all laws regarding the safe surrender of infants.

### Assessment & Criteria
=== "History of Present Illness"
    - Must be within the first 30 days of the child’s life

=== "Signs & Symptoms"
    - Child must have been delivered in a manner reasonably calculated to ensure the child’s safety
    - Surrendered to a hospital that provides 24-hour emergency services or to an attended rescue squad

=== "Differential Considerations"
    - Child Protective Services
    - Local Law Enforcement

### Pearls
* ⚠ Infant must be within first 30 days of life.
* ⚠ Virginia Safe Haven Law grants immunity from civil or criminal liability to receiving providers.
* Ensure safe and supportive environment for the surrendering parent if present.
