# Poquoson Fire and Rescue Patient Care Protocols
## Administrative - Transportation and Destination Determination

Respecting patient decision-making capacity while ensuring safe, appropriate medical transportation. This protocol balances patient hospital preference with the need to route time-critical conditions to specialized facilities.

### Assessment & Criteria
=== "History of Present Illness"
    - Patient decision-making capacity
    - Hospital preference stated by patient
    - Time of symptom onset

=== "Signs & Symptoms"
    - STEMI criteria met
    - Stroke/CVA criteria met
    - Other life-threatening time-critical conditions

=== "Differential Considerations"
    - Patient Preference vs. Closest Facility
    - Time-Critical Exceptions
    - HEMS availability and ETA


### Interventions
| # | Action / Intervention | EMR | EMT | A | I | P |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **Transport to patient choice:** Transport capable patients to the ED of their choice (if no exceptions) | | • | | • | |
| 2 | **Reroute for STEMI criteria:** Transport to STEMI Receiving Center per Poquoson Regional STEMI Plan | | • | | • | |
| 3 | **Reroute for Stroke criteria:** Transport to designated stroke center per Poquoson Stroke Triage Plan | | • | | • | |
| 4 | **Transport to closest:** Transport to the closest most appropriate hospital if no patient preference | | • | | • | |
| 5 | **Document reason:** Document reason for any reroute or destination change on the PCR | | • | | • | |
| 6 | **Contact HEMS:** Contact HEMS without Medical Control if beneficial to the patient | | • | | • | |
| 7 | **Initiate ground transport:** Initiate ground transport to closest hospital/helispot if HEMS > 10 min delay | | • | | • | |

### Pearls
1. ⚠ **Destination Changes:** Any time a patient is transported to the most appropriate facility other than the one requested, the reason for the change and destination shall be documented on the Patient Care Report (PCR).
2. ⚠ **Time-Critical Exceptions:** STEMI, Stroke/CVA, and other life-threatening conditions the AIC believes to be time critical override patient hospital preference to ensure specialized care.
3. ⚠ **HEMS Delays:** If the patient is ready for transport AND air ambulance is delayed >10 minutes, initiate ground transport to the closest hospital or helispot/helipad.
4. **Alternate Destinations:** If approved by the Poquoson OMD / Emergency Physician, alternate destinations may be established.
5. **Internal Policy:** Agencies shall have an internal policy explaining destination policy specific to that agency (i.e., time away from community limits).
