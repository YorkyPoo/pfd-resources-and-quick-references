# Poquoson Fire and Rescue Patient Care Protocols
## Administrative - STEMI Field Triage

This administrative protocol establishes the operational guidelines to rapidly identify patients experiencing an acute myocardial infarction and ensure safe, expedited transport to definitive care. Timely acquisition of a 12-lead ECG, appropriate routing, and early facility notification are critical to reducing time to intervention.

### Assessment & Criteria
=== "History of Present Illness"
    - Cardiac symptoms
    - High clinical suspicion of Acute MI

=== "Signs & Symptoms"
    - Machine interpretation reads "ACUTE MI" or "STEMI"
    - 1mm+ ST elevation in 2+ contiguous leads

=== "Differential Considerations"
    - Compromised airway
    - Impending circulatory collapse
    - Atypical or indeterminate ECGs


### Interventions
| # | Action / Intervention | EMR | EMT | A | I | P |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **Acquire 12-Lead ECG**<br>Must be completed within 5 minutes of patient arrival. | | • | | • | |
| 2 | **ECG Interpretation**<br>Identify STEMI based on cardiac symptoms AND 1mm+ ST elevation in 2+ contiguous leads. | | | | | • |
| 3 | **Routing - PCI Capable Hospital**<br>Transport to the closest PCI capable facility if EMS ground transport time is < 30 minutes. | | • | | • | |
| 4 | **Routing - HEMS Consideration**<br>If ground transport to a PCI facility is > 30 minutes, evaluate Helicopter EMS (HEMS) transport within 30 minutes. | | • | | • | |
| 5 | **Routing - Closest ED**<br>Transport to the closest ED (regardless of PCI capability) if the patient has a compromised airway or impending circulatory collapse. | | • | | • | |
| 6 | **Notifications**<br>Activate STEMI alert system to closest PCI center within 30 minutes. Call COR with "Pre-Hospital STEMI Alert" and patient Name/DOB. Transmit ECG and provide printed/electronic copy to facility/HEMS. | | • | | • | |
| 7 | **Serial ECGs**<br>For atypical/indeterminate ECGs with high suspicion, transmit ECG, contact Medical Control, and obtain serial ECGs every 10 minutes. | | • | | • | |

### Pearls
1. ⚠ Do NOT provide patient identifiers (Name/DOB) over the HEAR radio system. Call COR with "Pre-Hospital STEMI Alert" and identifiers.
2. Time targets are critical: Acquire 12-lead within 5 minutes of arrival, and activate the STEMI alert system to the closest PCI center within 30 minutes.
3. A printed or electronic copy of the ECG containing the patient's Name, DOB, and Time must be provided to the receiving facility or HEMS crew.
4. When in doubt regarding ECG interpretation or transport routing, contact Medical Control.
5. ⚠ Unstable patients (compromised airway or impending circulatory collapse) must be transported to the closest Emergency Department, regardless of PCI capability.
