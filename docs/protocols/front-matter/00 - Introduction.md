## Introduction

### Protocols, Policies, and Procedures Mission Statement

This document aims to provide current, well-researched, and accepted standards with the ultimate goal of minimizing the morbidity and mortality of our patients in Poquoson. It provides definitive guidelines for the treatment of specific emergency conditions in the pre-hospital setting by Poquoson Fire and Rescue personnel.

### Goals

- To establish minimum standards for appropriate patient care within Poquoson Fire and Rescue.
- To ensure an accountability structure for our Operational Medical Director, EMS leadership, and field providers.
- To provide a continuous quality improvement framework for our paramedic and EMT resources.

### Scope of Practice

These protocols apply exclusively to EMS providers operating under the medical direction of the Poquoson Fire and Rescue OMD. Providers are expected to utilize these protocols in conjunction with their clinical judgment, training, and the principles of Conscious Vigilance in patient care.
