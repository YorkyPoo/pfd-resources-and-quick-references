# **Diphenhydramine**
**Trade Name:** Benadryl

## Classification & Action
- **Drug Class:** Antihistamine, anticholinergic
- **Mechanism of Action:** Blocks histamine receptors. Causes mild sedation/drowsiness.

## Indications
- Allergic reaction
- Sedation
- Dystonic reaction

## Contraindications
- None

## Precautions
- May cause sedation
