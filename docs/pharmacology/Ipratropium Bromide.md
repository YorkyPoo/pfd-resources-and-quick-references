# [Ipratropium](../pharmacology/Ipratropium Bromide.md) Bromide
**Trade Name:** Atrovent

## Classification & Action
- **Drug Class:** Anticholinergic, Inhaled parasympatholytic
- **Mechanism of Action:** Causes bronchodilation. Dries respiratory secretions.

## Indications
- Respiratory Distress (Asthma/COPD)
- Inhalation Irritants

## Contraindications
- Known hypersensitivity/allergy

## Precautions
- May exacerbate glaucoma – use only when benefit outweighs risk
- May cause tachycardia – use with caution in patients with cardiac disease or ≥ 65 years old

## Dosage
### Adults
- **3 mL** Nebulized — For respiratory distress (per Airway protocol)

### Pediatrics
- **Not specified in protocol**
