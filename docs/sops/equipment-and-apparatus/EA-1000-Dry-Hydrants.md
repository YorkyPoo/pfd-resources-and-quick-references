IV. PROCEDURES

1. Shift Master Firefighters will determine which shift will be
responsible for each dry hydrant both spring and fall cycles.

2. After flushing, the master will enter the results into the
departments RMS.

3. The results should also note any deficiencies, missing signs,
or other items that would affect operations.

4. Notify Battalion once cycle is complete with any issues and
they will report to Fire Admin.

5. Fire Admin will then work on any items that need corrective
action(s).
