PORTABLE RADIOS

I. PURPOSE

To provide information and guidance on
the operation and functionality of the
Motorola XTS 2500 portable radio used
by this department.

This SOP is not all-inclusive and cannot
encompass all situations that may be
encountered.

II. POLICY

The City of Poquoson Fire/Rescue
Department is committed to provide and
maintain the safest working environment
possible in what can be the worst
conditions imaginable.

In this regard, reliable communications is
paramount. The proper use of our radio
hardware and software requires us to
continually train and evaluate our actions
during both emergency and non-
emergency operation in order to minimize
any problems.

Radio etiquette is also a very important
factor of our communication process.

III. RESPONSIBILITIES

It will be the responsibilities of each Company Officer to ensure that
all personnel under their command are familiar with and possess the
ability to utilize the various functions programmed into the Motorola
XTS 2500.

IV. PROCEDURES

The Motorola radios in use by the department allow for routine radio
traffic along with scanning of multiple channels.

e All personnel are assigned with a portable radio. The radio shall
be carried and be operational at all time.

e All personnel portable radios shall be accounted for each
morning by the Company Officer and be recorded in the Daily
Log.

e Portable Radios shall be secured when carried on the person.
This may be accomplished through the use of:

o Belt Clips

o Radio pocket on turnout coat
o Leather holster with shoulder strap.
