PUMP TESTING

I. PURPOSE

To establish a standard procedure for the
annual testing of fire apparatus pumps.
These tests are important indicators of
vehicle reliability.

This SOP is not all-inclusive and cannot
encompass all situations that may be
encountered.

II. POLICY

All Fire Department apparatus pumps
shall be tested once each year and after
any major repairs. This testing of pumps
shall be the "Annual Service Test."

Ill. RESPONSIBILITY
e It shall be the responsibility of the

Operations Officer to insure that the
Annual Service Tests are performed

on all pumping apparatus.
e The Operations Officer shall
ity Manager’s Signature schedule the testing of pumping

apparatus and coordinate with the
involved shifts.

e It shall be the responsibility of the Operations Officer to observe

e It shall be the responsibility of the Officer-In-Charge to provide
shift personnel to assist the Operations Officer during the
annual service test.

IV. PROCEDURES

e Service tests shall be conducted according to the procedures
described in NFPA Standard 1911 and according to the
manufactory’s recommendation.

e All tests shall be performed at a suitable drafting pit and tests
performed at draft.

e All main pumps on fire apparatus shall be tested.

e The entire Annual Service Test shall be re-instituted should a
failure occur on any one test resulting in repairs to the
apparatus.

e Apparatus undergoing annual pump testing shall be placed out-
of-service.

e Documentation will be retained at Fire Administration for
inspection by ISO representatives.
