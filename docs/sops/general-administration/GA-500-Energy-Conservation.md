ENERGY CONSERVATION

I. PURPOSE

To establish policy and guidelines relative
to the efficient use of energy and water.

This SOP is not all-inclusive and cannot
encompass all situations that may be
encountered.

II. POLICY

All Fire Department personnel shall:

e Strive to conserve both energy
(electricity) and water.

e Make every effort to curtail
unnecessary use of energy
(electricity) and water so as not to
give the appearance of waste.

III. GUIDELINES
Electricity
Lights/electrical equipment should be

turned off or placed in standby when not
being utilized.

Heating Systems

e All thermostats within fire stations shall be set and maintained
at 70 -72 degrees.

e All building doors will remain fully closed at all times.

e Apparatus bay doors will be closed immediately upon apparatus
exiting or entering bay and will remain closed at all times.

Cooling Systems

e Thermostats for controlling cooling equipment in fire stations
shall be set and maintained at 72-74 degrees.

e All building doors will remain fully closed at all times.

e Apparatus bay doors may remain open during warm days to
keep bay cool.

Water
In the event of water shortages:

e No private vehicles may be washed at fire stations.
e Training activities utilizing water are suspended.
