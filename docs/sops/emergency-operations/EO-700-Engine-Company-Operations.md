ENGINE COMPANY OPERATIONS

I. PURPOSE

To inform Fire Department Personnel of
the functions of the engine company.

II. APPLICABILITY
To career and volunteer personnel

Ill. OBJECTIVE

To provide a clear outline of duties to be
performed by crews assigned to the engine
company at the emergency scene. The
engine company is the primary unit in the
City of Poquoson Fire/Rescue. Consistent
use of these guidelines will assure the IC
that the initial tasks required to mitigate
the particular emergency are put into
action immediately upon arrival of the
engine company.

IV. GENERAL GUIDELINES

The Incident Commander and Company
Officers operating at multiple companies
and/or multiple agency emergencies shall
coordinate and integrate their efforts,
tasks, and functions so as to produce
harmonious, effective, and efficient

operations.

Incident Commanders shall endeavor to utilize the various fire
companies to their best advantage within the scope of their various
standard functions, but may, if the need arises, utilize companies for
any function, which may be required.
Fire companies must maintain a level of flexibility, which will ensure
their ability to perform the functions of other types of companies

(limited to available equipment and apparatus) as the situation
demands.

V. ENGINE COMPANY DUTIES

e Provide fire attack using hand lines or master streams

e Confine the emergency incident to the smallest area as safety,
resources, conditions, and time will allow

e Provide search and rescue of victims of an emergency incident
e Provide forcible entry

e Provide adequate and efficient water supply in hydrant and
non-hydrant areas to hose lines and other apparatus

e Provide emergency medical services to personnel and victims of
emergency incidents

e Provide RIT as directed

e Provide vehicle extrication and hazard control as directed on
MVA

e Provide assistance to local E.M.S. agencies on medical incidents

e Provide emergency and gross decontamination operations
