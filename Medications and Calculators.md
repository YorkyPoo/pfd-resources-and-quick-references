## Common Cardiology Dosages

- **Amiodarone:** 300mg IV/IO push (Cardiac Arrest - First Dose)
    
- **Epinephrine (1:10,000):** 1mg IV/IO every 3-5 mins (Cardiac Arrest)
    
- **Atropine:** 1mg IV/IO every 3-5 mins (Symptomatic Bradycardia)
